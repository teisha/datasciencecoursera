                  DATA DICTIONARY - average_data.csv

Comma-delimited file

Fields:

subjects - Numeric identifier of the subject wearing the device             
activity.label - Descriptive label of the activity the subject was performing when the measurement was taken       

The following fields are measurements from the devices
 - The letters X, Y, and Z represent the axis of the plane on which the measurement was taken.
 - Measurements will be either mean() or std()  (standard deviation).
 - Body Acceleration measurements subtract gravity from total acceleration.  Total acceleration is measured in standard gravity units "g".
 - Gravity Acceleration measurements are in standard gravity units "g".
 - Gyroscope Measurements show the angular velocity vector in radians/second.
